How Cryptic Comms Works

Cryptic Comms provides a secure and easy-to-use platform for encrypting and decrypting text using AES encryption. Simply go to the Encrypt page to enter your text and a password to create a secure encrypted message.

On the Decrypt page, you can paste an encrypted message and provide the correct password to unlock it. This ensures your sensitive information stays private and protected.

Using cutting-edge encryption technology, Cryptic Comms ensures that your files and messages remain safe from unauthorized access.
